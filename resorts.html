    A Shikara offers seating capacity for two men to five men. It is truly meant for cruising remote Alleppey back water villages which are not accessible by huge vessels. One can enjoy , terribly we mean it , shallow water cruise which call to mind the Amazon deep forest canals and would be a feather touch for one's bleeding heart by the ogling and hectic humanity as it allows a very close sight to the back waters , you can even dip your hand into the cool wonderful liquid what we call water, the back waters of Alappuzha in a shikara is quiet worthy. In a shikara you can closely watch the rustic lifestyle of back water villagesabsolutely genuine in its class.

    Alleppey Motor Boat Cruise       AHBVT03


    Motor boat is the another type of boat which is totally different from house boat. It is widely being used for the sightseeing and water transportation from one place to another, for the tourist who is having only limited number of hours. Half an hour journey by speedboat to the nearby island of Pathiramanal will reveal the range of rare migratory birds that fly from various parts of the world. The tourist can hire the motor boat at any time as they wish, but not in the night. It is available in different sizes for example two person to ten person , so the boat capacity will be 10 person , then ten to twenty so on...This is not a conducted trip on the other hand the tourist can hire one boat exclusively for them.
